# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Be respectful, evidence-oriented, and constructive. Contributions should improve prompt quality, attribution, reproducibility, or repository maintenance without harassing creators or misrepresenting their work.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discriminatory language, publishing private information, deliberate source fabrication, copyright abuse, and repeated disruption of review discussions.

## Enforcement

Report sensitive conduct concerns through the repository's [Security page](https://github.com/Evolink-AI/awesome-seedance-2.0-prompts/security) rather than posting private details in a public issue. Maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions and restrict participation when behavior threatens contributors or the project.

This policy applies to repository issues, pull requests, reviews, and other project-managed spaces.
